About Us

Wolf Software Systems Ltd builds powerful, reliable infrastructure tools in Rust. Our mission is to make server management accessible to everyone — from hobbyists running a home lab to enterprises managing hundreds of machines.

  • WolfScale — Database replication & load balancing

  • WolfNet — Encrypted mesh networking
  • WolfProxy — Reverse proxy with firewall
  • WolfServe — Apache2-compatible web server
