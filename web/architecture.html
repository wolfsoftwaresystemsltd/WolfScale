Elastic Performance Under Load

WolfScale handles burst writes gracefully with elastic replication:

  • Normal traffic ‚Äî Followers stay fully synchronized with the leader
  • Burst starts ‚Äî Followers may lag slightly as writes spike
  • Burst continues ‚Äî Lag stays stable, followers keep pace with leader
  • Burst ends ‚Äî Followers catch up quickly and return to zero lag

Actual lag depends on your server hardware and network speed. Followers maintain pace during bursts and catch up quickly when the burst ends. No data loss, no intervention needed.
