Can I cancel my Patreon at any time? ‚ñº
Yes, absolutely. Patreon subscriptions are month-to-month with no lock-in. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any point from your Patreon dashboard.

Patreon does require an account, but you can sign up in under a minute and pledge the minimum tier for a single month. Every little bit genuinely helps ‚Äî even a single month's contribution goes toward server costs.
